Our Story

Pan-Asian Cuisine in

Pacific Rim Bistro specializes in Pan-Asian cuisine. We offer a variety of nourishments ranging from the frozen tundra of Mongolia to the tropics of Thailand, to the craft of Japanese delights. Sushi lovers will be pleased — Pacific Rim Bistro is THE place downtown to find the freshest sushi and sashimi. We also have a lively bar offering an extensive wine list and signature cocktails!
